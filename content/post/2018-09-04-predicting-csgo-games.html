Context

As a big fan of CS:GO and analytics, I have built several prediction models to predict outcome of any given CSGO game. Documentation process is meant to remind me in the future of my thought process and techniques that I have used. The dataset is generated by scraping from hltv.org. Just from my understanding, here are the strongest variables that can influence a match outcome:

  • Teams strenght (I am estimating this using ELO Rating (chess system) based on team, line up and individual players) ??
  • Form of both teams (Recent resulsts)
  • Head to head records between 2 teams
  • Number of games played together for a given lineup
  • HLTV Rankings

For sake of time, will not write about preprocessing steps (very tedious).
